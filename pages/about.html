ABOUT US

Kaffeparty was founded with a simple mission: to bring coffee and chocolate lovers together in unforgettable tasting experiences. Our company specializes in organizing unique coffee and chocolate tasting events, where guests can discover the rich flavors and aromas of high-quality products from around the world. In addition to our tasting events, we offer a carefully selected range of premium coffee machines for home and professional use.

Each machine is chosen to provide the perfect brewing experience, combining quality, innovation, and design. Kaffeparty was born out of a passion for coffee culture and a desire to share it with others. From our first small tasting sessions, we have grown into a company dedicated to creating memorable experiences for both coffee enthusiasts and those new to the world of fine coffee and chocolate. Join us at Kaffeparty and explore the art of coffee, the joy of chocolate, and the pleasure of discovering new flavors together.
